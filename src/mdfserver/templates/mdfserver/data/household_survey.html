Results of the 2014 Household Survey

The 2014 iUTAH Household Survey was completed by over 2,400 households in 23 neighborhoods across 12 cities and 3 counties. We have been working hard this winter and spring to analyze the results and generate summary reports that can help local communities see how their residents use and feel about water in their area. Detailed Full Reports and Highlights Reports for each study area are available by clicking on the links below.  Maps of the neighborhoods where the study was conducted can also be seen by following the links at the bottom of the page.
