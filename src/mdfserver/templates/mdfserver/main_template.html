About Us

The iUTAH Modeling and Data Federation is a compendium of various tools and web-based interfaces for sharing data, models, and other digital resources. The iUTAH Cyberinfrastructure Team (CI Team) is focused on developing hardware and software systems and tools that improve iUTAH participantsâ€™ capacity for data collection, organization, management, sharing, synthesis to higher-level products, and integration with models.

Highlights

Time Series Data Access and Visualization: The iUTAH CI team has developed a web application for visualizing, summarizing, and exporting time series data. Currently, data from the iUTAH GAMUT network is accessible as well as active USGS sites in overlapping basins. Click here to visualize data with the web application.

Survey Data Viewer: We have developed a web-based tool for visualizing responses to social science surveys. The viewer allows users to easily explore survey data with a various view options and to differentiate responses between different demographic groups. The viewer has been implemented for the Utah Water Survey and other iUTAH surveys are being added. Click here to see the surveys.

Site and Watershed Pages: Dynamic content pages provide preview of current conditions and sites in the iUTAH GAMUT network.
Check out sites located in the Logan River, Provo River and Red Butte Creek watersheds.

Data Publication on the iUTAH Repository: For iUTAH researchers to publish data and models, we have deployed the iUTAH Repository. The Repository supports discovery and access of datasets to a wide audience and provides citations of data products results from iUTAH research efforts. Video tutorials for using the repository are available here.
